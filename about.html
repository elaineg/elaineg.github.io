About

Hi! My name is Elaine and I love being wrong. Although I was raised as an immigrant in a predominantly immigrant suburb in Vancouver, BC, I was shocked that only coming to Kingston, ON would teach me what true diversity felt like. The excitement of hearing new perspectives and challenging old beliefs is what gets me up at 6:30 every morning. My dream is to build a world where everyone seeks to question what they know.

Currently convinced that: Financial power should be decentralized, and we can start by teaching grade school students how to manage, save, and invest their money.

Currently reading: Professor Aswath Damodaran's blog about how to choose companies to invest in. He offers his complete Stern valuation course online free, giving anyone with wifi the power of an education.

Skills: Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Sketch, HTML/CSS, Javascript, Ruby
