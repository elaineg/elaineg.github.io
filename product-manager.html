Product manager for QShare

context

My university is located in Kingston, Ontario, which is a three-hour drive away from any major airport or Forever 21. It is costly to purchase train or bus tickets, so many students turn to carpooling as a cheaper alternative. However, the existing user journey to secure a carpool in and out of the city is full of pain points- It is done by sifting through unfiltered posts in a Facebook Group with 25,000 members. My QTMA product team wanted to fix that.

action

As product manager, I guided a senior and junior developer through product design sprints, development sprints, prioritized backlog tasks, and developed presentations with two junior analysts to communicate our product value to external stakeholders.

results

My team completed a live, functional MVP. QTMA hosted a demo day and invited product managers from Wealthsimple, Shopify, and TrendMicro to watch all product teams compete for imaginary venture capital. My product team received the most amount of funding.
