context

Every year, the AMS of Queen's University hosts an election to fill executive roles for the upcoming year. It is advertised widely through print and social media, reaching well over 27,000 undergraduate students.

action

In 2017, I volunteered to design Team JBP's visual identity and all the promotional graphics, which included as logos, banners, posters, pins, business cards, and endorsements. I was given two weeks' time on top of a full student courseload.
